EGG FRIED RICE

  • 1-2 eggs
  • 1/2-1 garlic
  • 1/4 onion
  • salt
  • pepper
  • oyster sauce
  • rice
  • Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat.
  • Saute onions and garlics for 2 to 3 minutes.
  • Pour in egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.
  • Stir in the cooked rice, mix well and sprinkle with oyster sauce and pepper.
